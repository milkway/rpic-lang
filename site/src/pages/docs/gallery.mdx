# Gallery

Figures from the compatibility corpus, **rendered by rpic when this page
was built**. The classics come from Brian Kernighan's pic manual and the
dpic distribution (J. D. Aplevich); the circuit figures reproduce
circuit_macros drawings through rpic's native library. Each card links to
its source.

<Gallery />

The full corpus — 72 manual/dpic figures and 48 circuit_macros figures,
with the parity matrix — lives in
[`examples/dpic`](https://github.com/milkway/rpic-lang/tree/main/examples/dpic)
and
[`examples/figuras`](https://github.com/milkway/rpic-lang/tree/main/examples/figuras);
full credits in
[ACKNOWLEDGMENTS.md](https://github.com/milkway/rpic-lang/blob/main/ACKNOWLEDGMENTS.md).
